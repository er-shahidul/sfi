The information SFI Implementation Committees (SICs) annually report to the Sustainable Forestry Initiative Inc. is of tremendous value in demonstrating SFI program improvement and impact. Your responses help us measure progress in achieving SIC goals and SFI Standard objectives and are a great vehicle to summarize SIC activities. SICs are encouraged to highlight key achievements and provide additional attachments. All files uploaded will be utilized for best practices sharing through the SIC Member's only area.
