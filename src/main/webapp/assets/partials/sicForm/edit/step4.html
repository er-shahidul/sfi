Did You Know?

VA SIC conducted a sustainable forestry tour to show architects and builders in the green-building community what SFI certification looks like on the ground. In addition, In partnership with the Virginia Forestry Association, the committee actively engaged in state legislature outreach about the importance of multiple green building rating systems, which in turn gives more options for increased use and recognition of SFI-certified wood products.
