SFI Market Survey

The reasons for certifying to the SFI Standards are as diverse as the more than 1,000 organizations that participate, and as diverse as the forests of North America. In order to ensure that your participation renders the value that you need and expect from SFI, it is important for us to understand your reasons for participating.

As an organization certified to one or more of the SFI standards, we would appreciate your perspective on the value of SFI certification and your involvement in the SFI program. SFI will use the results to generate insights and identify opportunities to better serve all SFI organizations certified to the SFI standards.

Please note that individual responses shall remain strictly confidential, and may only be used in aggregate form for marketing and communications purposes.
