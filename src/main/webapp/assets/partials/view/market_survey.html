8. Market Survey

SFI has an opportunity to engage with program participants on their activities, plans, opportunities and challenges while generating insights based on perceptions of market developments, emerging trends and opportunities for improvement. The responses to these questions may be used to generate management insights for SFI staff that will enable us to better serve the SFI community.
