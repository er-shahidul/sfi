WE STAND FOR FUTURE FORESTS
Welcome to the redesigned SFI annual progress report survey.

This survey addresses SFI Program requirements for annual reporting. There are also information-only questions outside the annual reporting requirements.

This is an opportunity for you to give SFI direct feedback on the value of your involvement in the program. Your response will remain confidential. We will use it to generate insights and ways to identify opportunities to better serve you and the larger SFI community. 

All questions are for the 2014 calendar year. Get started now.

Questions? Contact Rachel Dierolf (rachel.dierolf@sfiprogram.org). Visit SFI www.sfiprogram.org.

What is new in this year ...
SFI Annual Progress Report Survey
There are new questions in the survey, which are identified with (new!). As usual, new questions are not mandatory the first year asked. But next year these questions will be auditable. Here’s your chance to have a practice run.
Brand New Market Survey
This is a new and optional survey. We want to better understand your perceptions of the markets you work in. This is also an opportunity for you to give SFI direct feedback on the value of the program.
Standard Revision Questions
In 2016, we will ask about what difference the SFI 2015-2019 Standards have made. This year we have included the planned questions for 2016 so you can tell us how you have changed. You can use these questions to help track changes as you implement the standards this year.
Help Us Brag About You
Many survey questions will ask your permission to collect and attribute information so we can produce case studies. This is new. Previously, we only used aggregate data from the annual progress report survey. Case studies will help us showcase your successes.
