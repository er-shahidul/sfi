Privacy Policy
The data collected through the SFI annual survey is often used in aggregate in the SFI Progress Report.
SFI Program Participants must complete the SFI program participant survey annually to maintain good standing in the SFI® Program. New SFI Program Participants are allowed one year to report. The data submitted should be based on the best information available – which may mean using a best estimate, especially in the first years of implementation. Participants should use calendar year information.
All the information and data submitted is kept strictly confidential, and used only in aggregate form. If we wish to use participant-specific information as examples of noteworthy performance, we will seek permission.
